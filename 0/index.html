Overview

In this project, I explored the relationship between perspective, focal length, and camera positioning. The goal was to develop an intuitive understanding of how these factors affect image composition and visual perception through hands-on photography experiments.

Key Learning Objectives:

  • Understanding perspective distortion in close-up photography
  • Exploring architectural perspective compression effects
  • Creating the classic "dolly zoom" cinematographic effect
  • Analyzing the relationship between focal length, distance, and visual perception
